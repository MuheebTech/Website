SERVICES

Equipment Installation

Trouble shooting and repairs of kitchen, bakery and laundry equipment, testing and commissioning of Installed equipment. Installs electronic control panels and related mechanical or electrical equipment.

A/C Servicing

Air Conditioning Breakdowns can be frustrating and we understand your needs are urgent and important. Subject to your approval, minor repairs are carried out immediately and the air conditioner is back up and running

Painting And Wallpaper Fixing

Preparing painting surfaces by washing walls, repairing holes, or removing old paint. Mixing, matching, and applying paints and other finishes to various surfaces. Providing decorative and faux finishes as the project requires. Handling planning and prep work in an efficient manner.

POP and False Ceiling

Responsible for carrying out preparatory activities prior to installation of false ceiling and dry walls including measuring, marking, cutting, and fixing of the dry wall boards for dry wall installation.

Hardwood Flooring

Responsibilities are to meet with clients or contractors to determine the scope and budget of a flooring project, suggest appropriate wood materials and a timeframe for a job, and install a new floor or repair an existing floor.

Blinds and Curtains

Fitting blinds, drapery, traditional and motorized awnings, shutters, and similar coverings. Replacing components of clients' window coverings, if required. Applying frosting, tints, and appliques directly to windows.
